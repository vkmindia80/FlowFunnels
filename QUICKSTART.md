# FlowFunnels - Quick Start Guide

## üöÄ Get Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Register Your Account
1. Open FlowFunnels in your browser
2. Click "Sign up" on the login page
3. Enter your name, email, and password
4. Click "Create Account"

### Step 2: Explore the Dashboard
After registration, you'll land on your dashboard where you can:
- View all your funnels
- Create new funnels
- Browse templates
- Access analytics

### Step 3: Create Your First Funnel

**Option A: Start from Scratch**
1. Click "Create Funnel" button
2. Enter a funnel name (e.g., "My First Funnel")
3. Add a description (optional)
4. Click "Create Funnel"
5. Start adding pages to your funnel

**Option B: Use a Template (Recommended)**
1. Click "Browse Templates" button
2. Choose from 3 professional templates:
   - **Lead Capture Funnel** - Perfect for building your email list
   - **Product Launch Funnel** - Ideal for selling products
   - **Webinar Registration Funnel** - Great for hosting webinars
3. Click "Use Template" on your chosen template
4. The template will be cloned to your account
5. Customize it to match your brand

### Step 4: Build Your Funnel Pages
1. Open your funnel by clicking "Edit"
2. Click "Add Page" to create new pages
3. Enter page name (e.g., "Landing Page", "Thank You Page")
4. Configure page settings

### Step 5: Customize Content (Coming in Phase 2)
The drag-and-drop editor will allow you to:
- Add text, buttons, images, forms
- Customize colors and fonts
- Position elements anywhere
- Preview on mobile and desktop

### Step 6: Publish Your Funnel (Coming in Phase 3)
Once ready:
1. Click "Publish" button
2. Your funnel will be live
3. Share your funnel URL
4. Track performance in Analytics

## üìä Understanding Analytics

View real-time metrics for each funnel:
- **Page Views:** Total visitors to your funnel
- **Button Clicks:** Engagement with your CTAs
- **Form Submissions:** Leads captured
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of visitors who convert

## üí° Pro Tips

### Getting Started
- Start with a template to save time
- Keep your funnel simple at first
- Add pages one at a time
- Test your funnel before promoting

### Best Practices
- Use clear, compelling headlines
- Keep forms short (name + email)
- Add social proof when possible
- Include a clear call-to-action
- Test different variations

### Template Recommendations

**For Lead Generation:**
- Use the Lead Capture Funnel template
- Offer a free resource (ebook, guide, checklist)
- Keep the form simple (just name and email)
- Include a thank you page

**For Product Sales:**
- Use the Product Launch Funnel template
- Include video demonstration
- Add testimonials and guarantees
- Create urgency with countdown timers

**For Webinars:**
- Use the Webinar Registration Funnel template
- Highlight the webinar date/time
- List what attendees will learn
- Send confirmation email immediately
